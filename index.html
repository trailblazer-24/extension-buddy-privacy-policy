Capricorn Buddy ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our Chrome extension.

1. Information We Collect

Our extension may collect the following types of data:

1.1 Locally Stored Data

  • User Settings & Preferences: The extension stores site-specific settings, configurations, and user preferences in Chrome Storage for a better user experience.
  • Saved Commands: If you use the interactive command feature, relevant settings may be saved locally.

1.2 Downloaded & Imported Data

  • Exported Settings: When you export settings, the extension saves a file on your local device.
  • Imported Settings: If you import a settings file, it is only processed locally and not shared externally.

1.3 Interaction Data

  • Commands & Site Interactions: Any commands entered into the extension remain local to your browser and are not transmitted externally.
  • Base64 Encoding/Decoding: This tool runs entirely in your browser and does not send or receive data from external servers.
